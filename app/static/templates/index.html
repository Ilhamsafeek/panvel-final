Blockchain in CALIM 360

How Hyperledger Fabric Secures Your Contracts

Every contract lifecycle event is cryptographically hashed using SHA-256 and stored on our distributed Hyperledger Fabric network. The blockchain hash acts as an immutable digital fingerprintonce recorded, it cannot be altered without detection. This ensures complete transparency, legal validity, and QFCRA compliance.

How It Works - Step by Step

1

Create Contract

User creates or uploads a contract in CALIM 360

2

Generate Hash

System creates SHA-256 cryptographic hash of the document

3

Store on Blockchain

Hash is recorded on Hyperledger Fabric blockchain

4

Verify Anytime

Future verification compares current hash with blockchain record

Blockchain vs Traditional Database

Feature Traditional Database Logs Blockchain (Our System)
Tamper-proof Can be modified by admin Cryptographically immutable
Verification Trust-based Mathematically verifiable
Audit Trail Can be deleted/altered Permanent, distributed record
Single Point of Failure Yes, database can be compromised Distributed across multiple nodes
Regulatory Compliance May not meet QFCRA standards Meets Qatar blockchain requirements
Legal Validity Disputed in court Cryptographic proof accepted
